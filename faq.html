How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

Choose between HZ printer (A3, A4) and HZ plotter (A0, A1, A2)

Adjust the settings:

The default settings are:

  • black and white
  • Double sided
  • A4

First:

  1. Select: HZ printer
  2. Log into the printer using your pass or by logging in on the screen
  3. Then choose one of the following printer options:

Duplex printing (default):

  1. Go to end

Single-sided printing:

  1. Click on printer properties
  2. Go to the Basic Settings tab
  3. Select on option 1-sided/2-sided/Booklet Printing, 1-sided printing
  4. Go to end

Colour printing:

  1. Click on printer properties
  2. Go to the Basic Settings tab
  3. Select on color mode, color
  4. Go to end

Automatic stapling:

  1. Click on printer properties
  2. Go to the Basic Settings tab
  3. Select on Finishing for Stapling + Collation, Staple + Collate
  4. Go to end

End

  1. Choose the print release option
  2. After printing, tap the icon in the top right to log out
How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?
  1. Log into the printer using your pass or by logging in on the screen
  2. Choose the scan option in the menu
  3. Place your documents on the glass plate

Double-sided scanning:

  • Press change settings
  • Select on duplex-mode, 2-sided
  1. Press start
  2. After scanning, tap the icon in the top right to log out

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

Free parking near HZ

Free parking is available at the car park at Poelendaelesingel 12 in Middelburg. This is at a few minutes' walk from HZ. Unfortunately, it is not possible to park directly at the HZ premises. However, there is a disabled parking space available right next to the HZ building at Het Groene woud 1-3.

Car park Poelendaelesingel

At the barrier of the car park the Poelendaelesingel you can ring the bell. After passing the barrier, follow the signs to the HZ parking places, marked with a white sign with the HZ logo.

With HZ pass or ring the bell

Students and staff can only enter by presenting their HZ pass at the barriers, guests can ring the bell at the barrier.

Walking to HZ location

If you park at the Poelendaelesingel you can reach the HZ via the pedestrian tunnel.
