Why did I choose ict?

  • Digital is what I love
  • I want to help on software projects
  • I love open source
  • I like linux
  • ICT connects all my interests together
  • I am a nerd

The beginning

When I was little I loved video games, but it was mainly my dream to play them online. Then when I was allowed to I didn't only learn about games. I also learned about the world wide web with a wide array of content to explore. I didn't understand the inspect element and I didn't know I had the possibility to do it myself. But it facinated me. When I became older I mostly played retro video games we had and fixed them for my current computer using hex editors. I didn't know, but this was my first introduction to coding.

What came inbetween

In high school I was mainly distracted with my tests. The computer I used back before my high school started was thrown out. So I only had old laptops that my family didn't use, the current one I have is my brothers old laptop. Because they were old laptops and desktop environments moved on, I was done listening to the loud fans running and my laptop screaming just to run windows 7 with it's base applications. After searching for solutions I found the magic of linux which could fix my problem. I started using ubuntu as my first operating system as this seemed like the best beginner option. After some time I kept distro hopping which made me more experienced with different distributions, this also made me understand differences that seem minor at first, but are quite impactful after a while. Due to the operating system I used I began to find the value in privacy and freedom. This in turn made my love for linux, open source and just computers in general bigger. I never knew what I wanted to do for my study, but I wouldn't know any other place then ICT. Thus I was looking around in the ICT studies and landed on ICT at Avans, HZ or game development at BUAS. Avans was just way to big and unpersonal for me, it was badly orgized as well, so that one isn't school I want to end up in. I found out that the study at BUAS would make me and up at some triple A studio and this is not a place I want to work, unless I hate myself. I want to have a life so it wouldn't be a study that I would want to follow. It would've had more of a chance if the projects to get into the school weren't planned just before my exams.

Now I am here!

I finished my high school and now started with my study of ICT at HZ University of Applied sciences. I couldn't be happier with the choice that I made. I already met great people and haven't ever felt in place as much as I do here. The start was rough, I was using opensuse which challenged my knowledge of apache and the operating system itself. It didn't work out which made me change to windows. Then as a kind arrival gift, windows wrote inside my MBR which corrupted the GRUB. Now I changed to mx Linux which is a derivitive of Debian. Debian itself is to special for me and MX makes linux work.
